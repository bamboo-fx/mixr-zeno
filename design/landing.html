HOW IT WORKS

From DM to dance floor in 3 steps.

01

Send a mixer request

Pick a group, propose a date, suggest themes. The other group accepts, counters, or declines.

02

Everyone votes

Members from both groups RSVP and vote on the theme + activity. Results lock at your deadline.

03

Show up & rate it

Check in, see your pairings, post stories. After the mixer, both groups rate the night.
